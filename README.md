## <i class="fas fa-circle-info" style="color:#6CA2C6"></i> About this site

Math Kickstarter is an online refresher course covering foundational mathematics topics. It is designed for students who need to strengthen their skills before — or during — their engineering programme.

The site contains **short video lectures**, **self-check quizzes**, and **exercises with solutions** organised into five topic areas.

!!! warning "Have you taken the pre-test?"

    This site is intended for students who scored **below 75%** on the mathematics pre-test. If you have not taken the test yet, please do so before continuing: **[Take the pre-test](https://forms.office.com/e/x9B3SYivqr)**.

    See [Getting Started](/math_kickstarter/pages/getting_started/) for full details.

---

## <i class="fas fa-route" style="color:#6CA2C6"></i> How to use this site

1. Complete the **pre-test** and identify which topics you need to review.
2. Open the relevant topics from the table below (or the **Topics** menu).
3. Watch the videos, take the quiz, and work through the exercises for each topic.

If you need several topics, we suggest this order: Basic Arithmetic and Equations → Functions → Differential Calculus → Integral Calculus → Vectors in 2D.
