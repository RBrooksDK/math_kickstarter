### General information

??? question "What is Math Kickstarter?"
    Math Kickstarter is a self-paced online brush-up course covering foundational mathematics topics for engineering students at VIA University College. It includes short videos, self-check quizzes, and exercises with solutions.

??? question "Who should use this site?"
    This site is intended for students who scored **below 75%** on the mathematics pre-test. If you scored 75% or above, you generally do not need to work through this material.

??? question "Do I need to complete every topic?"
    No. Use the pre-test results to identify which topic areas you should review. Focus on those topics rather than working through the entire site from start to finish.

??? question "What topics are covered?"
    The site covers five areas: Basic Arithmetic and Equations, Functions, Differential Calculus, Integral Calculus, and Vectors in 2D. See the topic table on the [home page](/math_kickstarter/).

??? question "Are advanced calculus techniques included?"
    No. This is a brush-up course. Topics such as the product, quotient, and chain rules for differentiation, and integration by substitution or partial fractions, are deliberately excluded.

---

### Using the site

??? question "In what order should I watch the videos?"
    Within each topic, watch the videos in the order they appear on the page. If you need several topics, we recommend starting with Basic Arithmetic and Equations and progressing through to Vectors in 2D.

??? question "What does a quiz score of 7/10 mean?"
    The quizzes are for self-assessment only. A score of at least 7 out of 10 suggests you have sufficient understanding of the material in that topic. Results are not stored or reported.

??? question "Where are the exercise solutions?"
    Solutions are available on each topic page. Click the answer blocks to reveal the solution.
