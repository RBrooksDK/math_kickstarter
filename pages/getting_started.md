## Before you begin: the pre-test

This website is intended for students who need a **math brush-up** before or during their engineering programme. It is **not** a replacement for the full mathematics curriculum — it covers selected foundational topics through short videos, quizzes, and exercises.

!!! warning "Have you taken the pre-test?"

    Before using this site, you should complete the **mathematics pre-test** provided by your programme.

    - If you score **75% or above**, you generally do **not** need to work through this material.
    - If you score **below 75%**, you should use this site to review the topics where you need support.

    Your pre-test result will indicate which topic areas to focus on. Follow those recommendations rather than working through every topic from start to finish.

    **[Take the pre-test here](https://forms.office.com/e/x9B3SYivqr)**

---

## How to use this site

1. **Take the pre-test** and note which topic areas you should review.
2. Open the relevant topic(s) from the **Topics** menu or the table on the [home page](/math_kickstarter/).
3. **Watch the videos** in the order shown on each topic page.
4. **Take the quiz** at the bottom of the topic page. A score of at least **7 out of 10** suggests sufficient understanding of the material.
5. **Work through the exercises**. Use the collapsible answers to check your work.

You can revisit any topic at any time. Quizzes are for self-assessment only — results are not stored or reported.

## What this course covers

The site is organised into five topic areas:

| Topic | Subtopics |
| ----- | --------- |
| [Basic Arithmetic and Equations](/math_kickstarter/basic_arithmetic_and_equations/) | Order of operations, parentheses, exponents and radicals, fractions, equations, quadratic equations, rearranging formulae |
| [Functions](/math_kickstarter/functions/) | Functions and graphs, common function types, inverse functions |
| [Differential Calculus](/math_kickstarter/differential_calculus/) | Derivatives and tangents, differentiation rules (basic) |
| [Integral Calculus](/math_kickstarter/integral_calculus/) | Indefinite integrals, definite integrals and area under curves |
| [Vectors in 2D](/math_kickstarter/vectors/) | Vectors in the plane, dot product, parametric equation of a line |

Some advanced techniques are **deliberately excluded** (for example the product, quotient, and chain rules for differentiation, and integration by substitution or partial fractions). The focus is on the core skills needed for engineering mathematics.
